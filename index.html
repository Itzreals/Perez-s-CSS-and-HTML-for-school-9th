Parkour Reborn

PARKOUR Reborn is a Roblox parkour game by A Mongoose Studio, a remake of PARKOUR Legacy, released on November 3rd, 2023

Players explore a city called Verna, using parkour to complete missions, challenges, and races. Key features include the ability to collect resources to craft gear, discover new movement abilities, and master complex techniques in an evolving open world. The game is currently in a paid alpha stage but will become free upon its public beta release.
